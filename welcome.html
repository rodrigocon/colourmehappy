About Us

by Tale
published on October 21, 2022

Sydney social paint, ink and draw!!

Some people think that art is a therapy in itself - what is more healing than a moment to express your creativity and have fun while doing it :)

Let's gather and practice some painting, inking and drawing. By bringing together different interests we can enrich ourselves with creative ideas that can further spark our imaginations.

But wait! That's not all. Once we have had fun doing it, we want to meet each other, and see if we want to come back again, and make friends that craft together, or friends for life.
